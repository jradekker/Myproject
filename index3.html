What we do

As Friends of Chernobyl's Children Foundation, we help children in the region that still has been affected by the Chernobyl disaster.

Some of them get structural financial support.

They take part in our adoption programme.

Most of the kids get financial support on an irregular base.

The donations we get, are used for this goal.
